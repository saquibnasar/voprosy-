(Note to author) If you select this path you may submit your submission free of charge. As we receive large volumes of submissions weekly your paper will be queued – the first step  is the Preliminary Assessment by our Review Board who will decide if your paper will go into Full review. This Preliminary Assessment may take 3-7 weeks. Once the Review Board has Assessed your paper you will be notified.
